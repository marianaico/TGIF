About TGIF

Transparent Government in Fact (TGIF) is an organization dedicated to providing citizens with easy access to information about their representatives in Congress. We believe that transparency is the foundation of a healthy democracy.

Use this website to explore data on the current members of the Senate and the House of Representatives, filter them by political party or state, and see their voting records and years in office.

The composition and powers of Congress are established in Article One of the U.S. Constitution. The Senate and the House of Representatives work together to create laws that affect the entire nation.
